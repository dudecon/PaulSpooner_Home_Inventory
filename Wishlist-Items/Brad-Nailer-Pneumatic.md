\# Pneumatic 18-Gauge Brad Nailer (Planned)



Desired for clean finish and trim work (molding, baseboards, cabinetry, paneling) where framing nailer would leave larger holes/marks.



\*\*Why add this\*\*

\- Complements existing \[\[Framing-Nailer]] for full pneumatic range: framing (3"+ nails) + finish (smaller brads).

\- Pneumatic preferred over cordless for your air-compressor setup (consistent power, no battery fade).

\- Current pancake compressor can drive it for short jobs; upgrade compressor will make it ideal for longer sessions.



\*\*Shopping notes\*\*

\- Focus on 18-gauge (most versatile for home/DIY).

\- Look for models with good reviews for reliability and minimal jamming.

\- Check local stores (Home Depot, Harbor Freight in Coeur d'Alene area) or online for deals.
