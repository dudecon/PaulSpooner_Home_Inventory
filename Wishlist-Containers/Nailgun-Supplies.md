\# Nailgun Supplies Bin



Dedicated organizer bin (e.g., plastic tote, stackable bin, or 5-gal bucket with lid) for pneumatic framing \& brad nailer consumables, accessories, and quick-use tools. Keeps everything together near \[\[Pancake-Air-Compressor]] or future \[\[30Gal-Wheeled-Air-Compressor]] zone.



\## Quick Contents List (no separate files yet)

contents:

\- Framing nails 21° plastic-collated (\~3" to 3-1/4" common for framing; stock \~5–10 lbs assorted)

\- Framing nails 21° plastic-collated (\~2-1/2" to 3" sinkers or ring-shank for subfloor/sheathing)

\- Brad nails 18-gauge (\~5/8" to 2" assorted lengths; e.g., 1", 1-1/4", 1-1/2", 2" for trim/molding)

\- Pneumatic tool oil (e.g., 4–8 oz bottle; add before each use to prevent dry firing damage)

\- Small hammer (e.g., 8–12 oz finishing hammer for setting proud nails)

\- Nail set / flush-punch set (1/32", 1/16", 1/8" for driving sub-flush nails without marring)

\- Wood filler (e.g., stainable latex or epoxy putty for filling brad/framing holes)

\- Putty knife / flexible scraper (2–4" for applying filler smoothly)

\- Safety glasses (foam-rimmed or impact-rated ANSI Z87.1)

\- Earmuffs / hearing protection (NRR 25–30 dB for compressor + nailing noise)

\- Quick-connect air fittings (1/4" NPT male/female couplers + spare set if hose lacks them)

\- Air hose whip (short 1–3 ft leader hose to reduce strain on tools)

\- Rags / shop towels (for wiping oil, dust, or oil from tools)

\- Spare O-rings / rebuild kit basics (common wear items for nailers)

\- Small parts tray or magnetic dish (for loose nails/fittings during jobs)

\- Nail storage clips / bags / organizers (to keep sizes separated and dry)



\*\*Notes\*\*

\- Stock nails in original boxes or ziplock bags labeled by size/type.

\- Oil the nailers after every few hundred shots or per manual.

\- Add to \[\[Ideal-Pneumatic-Workshop]] blueprint once acquired.

\- When buying: look for bulk nail packs at Home Depot/Lowe's in Coeur d'Alene for best value.
